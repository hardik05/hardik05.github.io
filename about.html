About Hardik

Security Researcher, Trainer, and Fuzzing Expert.

Hardik Shah

Principal Malware Reverse Engineer

Biography

With over two decades of dedicated experience in the security research field, Hardik is a seasoned expert in vulnerability discovery and malware reverse engineering. His journey began in Indore (M.P.), where he earned a Masters in Computer Application from SGSIPS, laying the groundwork for a career at the forefront of cybersecurity.

Currently, he serves as a Principal Malware Reverse Engineer at Palo Alto Networks in Bangalore. His career has been shaped by significant roles at leading security firms, including a decade at McAfee, and impactful tenures at Vehere, Sophos, Symantec, and ClearTrail Technologies.

This extensive background has provided him with deep expertise in fuzzing, root cause analysis, and exploit analysis across diverse platforms. He is a firm believer in continuous learning and is passionate about sharing knowledge through training. When he is not hunting for the next 0-day, he enjoys spending quality time with his two children.
