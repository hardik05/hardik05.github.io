Biography

I belong to Indore (M.P.) and completed my Masters in Computer Application from SGSIPS, Indore. I currently work as a Principal Malware Reverse Engineer at Palo Alto Networks, Bangalore, India (since Feb 2025).

Previously, I worked with Vehere for 1.11 years (Remote, Bangalore), Sophos for 1.2 years (Bangalore), McAfee for 10 years (Bangalore), Symantec for 3.5 years (Pune), and ClearTrail Technologies (Indore) for 2 years.

I have over 20+ years of experience in Security Research. I believe in constant learning, and when I'm not working, I spend my time with my two kids.
