Training Philosophy

Hardik believes in a "hands-on-keyboard" approach. While theory provides a crucial foundation, true mastery in security research is forged by doing. His workshops are designed to be intensely practical, bridging the gap between concepts and real-world application as quickly and efficiently as possible.

The focus isn't just on talking about finding bugs, but on finding, analyzing, and understanding them. His goal is to demystify the process of vulnerability discovery and empower you with the skills and confidence to find your own 0-days. The best way to learn is by breaking things, and his training provides a safe, structured environment to do just that, with expert guidance to help you connect the dots from crash to CVE.

"Fuzzing is not magic; it's persistent, structured execution."
